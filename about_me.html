About

Like raindrops meeting the surface of still water, my journey unfolds in concentric circles of awareness and understanding. Each moment brings new insights that ripple outward, touching all aspects of being.

In the space between thoughts, I find the clarity of empty luminosity—a state of mind where wisdom and compassion naturally arise. This practice guides my interactions with the world and informs my approach to life's ever-changing circumstances.

Just as the pool receives each raindrop without judgment or resistance, I aspire to remain open to new connections and conversations. If my path resonates with yours, I welcome the opportunity to share ideas and experiences through mindful dialogue.
